Overview

This project explores how design can support families in staying emotionally connected despite busy schedules and physical distance. Through research, I found that family communication often relies on occasional calls and urgent text messages, which makes meaningful connection difficult to sustain in everyday life. In response, I designed an app concept that encourages bite-sized daily interactions under 10 minutes, offering a lighter and more sustainable way for family members to stay involved in one another’s lives.

This project was taken under the NUS module — DTK1234: Design Thinking.
